Manchester Arndale

The arndale shopping centre, stationed on market street, is the perfect place for a shopping spree. It has over 200 stores, such as technology giants like ‘Apple’, as well as clothing stores like ‘H&M’, ‘New Look’ and more. There is also an array of food stores and restaurants to explore whilst you are shopping.

The Lowry

The Lowry shopping mall, is located in Salford quays with amazing views of the Manchester ship canal. It is one of the most famous designer outlet malls in Manchester, and offers amazing products at discounted prices.
