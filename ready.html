Overview

Explore your options and different types of accommodation in Manchester. Once you’ve organised your living arrangements check out our useful moving in checklist. Here you'll also find more about the ResLife team in halls of residence and our Living at Home society to help get you settled. We've also got some information about staying safe in your local area and how to be a valued member of your new community.

You will need a valid email address to complete the application and a valid University of Manchester ID number which will be on your offer letter. if you change your mind about where you will be living, it is okay becauase your money is refundable!

Tips and Tricks

  • Buy second hand! I found all my cutlery and crockery in charity shops which saved me SO much money, and my second hand John Lewis finds were far better quality than I would have ever got otherwise.
  • Chances are your new bedroom won't be too big, and your storage space won't be either. Pack sensibly and remember you'll be moving out again in June so it's not really that long.
  • Don't leave packing until the last minute – it takes longer than you think!
  • Check what your halls provide first – you don't want to end up with double of everything.
