Studying In Manchester

John Rylands Libary

The Victorian library has everything including rich architecture, knowledge, and culture. If you are looking for a change of scenery for a place to study, you can find a quiet spot in the historical library and continue your studying. Although it is located off campus in deansgate, it is definitely worth the visit.

Directions



Main Libary

The University Library is one of the greatest places to study, either by yourself or with a group of friends. It has group study spaces where you can book rooms to collaborate on projects or you can book individual spaces for those looking for quieter areas to work in. The library has long opening hours so you can stay for as long as you like.

Directions



Central Libary

This five floor building is the oldest and largest public library in Manchester, where you can find and sit in a beautiful hall with study spaces to complete coursework or maybe to do some revision. The library also has some group study spaces where you can book to complete group projects.
