Course Overview

For students entering into the foundation year it is important to recognize all the demands that come with it. This will be a quick overivew of what to expect over the first and second semesters of your study during the computer science foundation year.

Previous Next
Academic Year Breakdown (2022-2023)
Semester 1
Mathematics OB1 Mathematics ON1 Academic Skills Information and Communications Technology Physics 1 Physics 2 Chemistry 1 Mathematics OC1
Semester 2
Mathematics OB2 Mathematics OD2 Mathematics OJ2 Mathematics OF2 Computational Thinking Foundation Year Project Mathematics OC2

It is advisable for students who are planning to take a foundation year in computer science to prepare themselves beforehand to make the process easier to handle in the long term especially if a student has not studied or come across any of the concepts listed in each unit. It would help to check these resources and practice concepts shown to increase your potential to succeed in the computer science foundation year.
