Physics 1 - Matter and Energy

This unit will cover all topics under the umbrella of Matter and Energy. Here is what you can expect to see in this unit

  1. Physical Quantities
  2. Dimensional Analysis
  3. Problem Solving Skills
  4. Transfer and Conservation of Energy
  5. Temperature and Heat
  6. Pressure in Fluids
  7. Ideal Gas
  8. Kinetic Theory
  9. Thermodynamics
  10. Deformation of Matter
    11.

The course unit is structured following weekly-based content that follows concepts related to matter and energy.

What is achieved from completion:

  • An understanding of diagrammatic, graphical, numerical, symbolic and verbal forms
  • Knowledge and understanding of the fundamental principles with regard to topics covered
  • Mathematical knowledge and skills into the study of physics
    •

Learning Physics 1 gives you a range of subject-specific skills that can be applied in the following areas like Metallurgist, Geophysicist, Academic researcher e.t.c.

The recommended study hours for this unit is:

  • Assesment written exam - 2
  • Lectures - 24
  • Tutorials - 11
  • Independent study - 63
