Getting Groceries In Manchester

Tesco

This supermarket is essential for students living in Manchester, as there are plenty of shops that are very conveniently located. You can find a Tesco express almost anywhere in Manchester, namely on Wilmslow road and Upper Brook street. This is a go-to for most students as there are many discounted grocery items, thanks to their clubcard scheme, that makes grocery shopping a lot cheaper.

Lidl

Crowned as the UK’s cheapest supermarket by the company ‘Which?’, Lidl offers low prices for your everyday items. The average cost for 45 items comes down to only £42.67 (as of 2020]#). Low prices for quality goods, such as fresh produce and baked goods, means this supermarket could be the best option for your weekly grocery shop. There are Lidl stores located on Wilmslow road, Stanley grove, and Picadilly.

Last updated 3 mins ago
ASDA

Asda is considered the UK’s third cheapest supermarket and offers a wide range of products due to their larger stores. There are two superstores local to student areas however, asda offer a very accessible home delivery service. Many students take advantage of this convenient service as it saves time and allows those who usually rely on public transport to purchase a larger grocery order.
