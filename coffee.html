Getting Coffee in Manchester

Black Sheep Coffee

The stunning, rustic decor and beautiful aromas gives this coffee shop a relaxing vibe that is perfect for studying. You can simply order yourself on a screen or order at the counter, and in a few minutes you can enjoy their delicious hot drinks. This coffee shop can be found all over Manchester in the following locations: St Peter’s square, Spring gardens, Piccadilly station, and Deansgate.

Idle Hands

This independent business can be found in the northern quarter, and it serves some amazing coffee made with a wide range of coffee and brewing methods. This high quality coffee can also be enjoyed with some baked goods, as this coffee shop also specialises in making pies.

Locations



Takk

You can find this nordic inspired independent coffee company in three locations around manchester: tariff street, the hatch, and the university green on oxford road. Whether you’re looking for a quick shot of espresso made from high quality coffee or a delicious meal made from fresh vegetables, meat and dairy, you can find it at Takk.
