My Lahore

Located in the heart of Manchester's famous Wilmslow road (a place known for its mouth-watering south asian and middle eastern cuisine) with a menu that caters to all. Their dishes range from traditional asian curries to western style burgers and pizza, providing a true british-asian experience.

Shoryu Ramen

Shoryu Ramen specialises in delicious and authentic ramen noodles, made to your liking, in a rich soup accompanied by some of Japan’s best side dishes and street food. Shoryu brings a traditional japanese experience, specifically from the Hakata region of Fukuoka city, rarely found outside of Japan to Manchester’s Piccadilly gardens.

Tattu

Offers a modern take on traditional Chinese cuisine and flavours such as wagyu dim sum and chinese barbeque chicken. The dark wooden interior and detailed carved screens combined with a beautiful cherry blossom tree provide a refined and cultured ambience. Although it is located in Manchester’s spinningfield, it will transport you on a sensory journey to the east.

Don Giovanni

This establishment is Manchester’s oldest independently owned Italian restaurant and is situated on oxford street. Their menu, which ranges from homemade lasagne to their exquisite desserts, is enriched with high quality authentic meals made from fresh ingredients. The elegant yet cosy decor creates the perfect environment for an evening meal.
