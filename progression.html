Progression to first year

The average rate of progression to First Year Computer Science is 73%.

13% of students progressed to other degrees, while

14% of them couldn't progress.

Grades Required for Computer Science

Super Units Course 1 Course 2 Course 3 Grade (%)
Super Unit 1 Math 0B1 / 0C1 Math 0B2 / 0C2 Math 0F2 75%
Super Unit 2 Optional 1 Optional 2 Comp. Thinking 60%
Super Unit 3 Math 0N1 Math 0J2 Math 0D2 70%
Super Unit 4 ICT Academic Skills Group Project 65%

More Information about Progression Rules

  • Optional courses are Physics 1, Physics 2 and Chemistry.
  • All units averages must be over 50%. (1 unit in each Super Unit can be compansated if it is over 40% except for Super Unit 4)
  • In the Super Unit 4, all components must be over 50%.
  • Courseworks usually constitute 20-30% of each unit.
  • Total coursework average must be over 50%.
  • Resit exams are capped at 50%.
